Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-11-17

Thank you for using Locale Browser! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and how third-party services integrated into the app may collect and process data.

Data Collection and Use

Locale Browser does not collect or store any personal user information. However, the app includes third-party services that may collect data as part of their functionality:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. AdMob may collect device information, location data, or other identifiers for personalized advertising. For more information, please refer to the Google AdMob Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to analyze app usage and performance. Firebase may collect device identifiers and usage data. For details, see the Firebase Privacy Policy.

Managing Ad Preferences

If you wish to opt out of personalized ads, you can manage your ad preferences through your device settings:

  • Android: Go to Main Screen > More (3 dots) > Privacy Settings.

Children's Privacy

Locale Browser is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. If we become aware that personal information has been inadvertently collected from a child, we will take steps to delete such data promptly. Please contact us if you believe this has occurred.

Data Security

While Locale Browser does not directly collect data, the integrated third-party services apply industry-standard security practices to protect any collected information.
