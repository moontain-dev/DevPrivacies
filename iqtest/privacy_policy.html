Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Moomtain ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our IQ Test Assistant application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Test Results and Progress: Your test answers, scores, completion time, and progress are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Through Firebase Analytics, we collect anonymous data about how you use the App, including:
    • App feature usage patterns
    • Test completion rates
    • Time spent on different sections
    • App performance metrics
  • Crash Reports: Through Firebase Crashlytics, we collect crash reports to improve App stability.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the App's functionality
  • Storing your test history and results
  • Analyzing App usage to improve user experience
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Delivering relevant advertisements

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics: For App usage analysis
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting

Data Storage and Security

Your test results and progress are stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information, but please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Advertisements

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may use device identifiers and location data to serve personalized ads. You can adjust your device's advertising settings to control personalized advertising.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored test results
  • Delete your test history from the device
  • Opt out of analytics data collection through your device settings
  • Control personalized advertising through your device's advertising settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
