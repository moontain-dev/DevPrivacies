Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Moomtain ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our IQ Test Assistant application (the "App").

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: We collect and process data only with your explicit consent, which you can withdraw at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process some data to maintain and improve our services, ensuring they function properly and securely.
  • Contract: Some data processing is necessary to provide you with our services as requested.

Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Test Results and Progress (Local Storage):
    • Test answers and scores
    • Completion time
    • Progress data
    • This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
  • Usage Data (With Consent): Through Firebase Analytics, we collect:
    • App feature usage patterns
    • Test completion rates
    • Time spent on different sections
    • App performance metrics
  • Crash Reports (With Consent): Through Firebase Crashlytics, we collect:
    • App crash information
    • Technical error details
    • Device information related to crashes

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the App's functionality
  • Storing your test history and results locally
  • Analyzing App usage to improve user experience (with consent)
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Delivering relevant advertisements (with consent)

Data Transfers

When you consent to analytics and crash reporting, data may be transferred to servers located in the European Union and the United States. These transfers are protected by:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Google's Data Processing Terms
  • Appropriate security measures

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. With your consent, we may share anonymous, aggregated data with:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics: For App usage analysis
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting

Data Storage and Security

Your test results and progress are stored locally on your device. For data processed with your consent:

  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures
  • We use industry-standard security protocols
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

Please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Advertisements

With your consent, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. You can:

  • Control personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Withdraw your consent for personalized ads at any time
  • Choose to see only non-personalized ads

Your GDPR Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: View your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us directly.

Data Retention

We retain different types of data for different periods:

  • Local test results: Until you delete them
  • Analytics data: 14 months
  • Crash reports: 90 days

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 16 years of age in the EU. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes and seek your consent again if necessary. The new Privacy Policy will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
