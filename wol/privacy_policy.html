Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to WakeOnLan (Magic Packets). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • Device Information: Names and MAC addresses of devices you add to the app for Wake-on-LAN functionality
  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you use the app, including:
    • Number of devices added
    • Wake-up attempts and their success rates
    • App crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing the core Wake-on-LAN functionality
  • Improving app performance and reliability
  • Debugging and crash analysis
  • Understanding user needs and preferences

Data Storage

All device information is stored locally on your device. We do not store your devices' MAC addresses or names on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: For basic app usage analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting and app stability monitoring

These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their respective privacy policies.

These services DO NOT collect your devices' MAC addresses.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Device data is stored securely on your local device, and crash reports are transmitted securely to our analytics service.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete any device data you've added to the app
  • Opt out of analytics collection through your device settings
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
