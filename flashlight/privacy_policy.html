Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to Flashlight ("we", "our", or "app"). We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you use our app, including features you use (such as flashlight, screen light, flashing modes, etc.), frequency of use, and app settings.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, app version, and device settings.
  • Crash Reports: When the app crashes, we collect crash reports to help us improve the app's stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our app and services
  • Develop new features and enhance user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers that help us provide services, such as Google Firebase (for analytics and crash reporting). These service providers may only process your information according to our instructions.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices and Controls

You can control the data we collect through the app's settings menu:

  • Data Collection Toggle: You can enable or disable data collection in the app's settings. Disabling this feature will stop the collection of analytics data and crash reports.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Third-Party Services

Our App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics provided by Google, Inc. These services may collect and process certain information about your device and App usage. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how Google handles your data.
