Privacy Policy

SimLocation – GPS Location Simulator

Effective Date: April 21, 2025  |  Developer: Moomtain

📋 1. Introduction

Welcome to SimLocation ("the App"), a GPS location simulation tool developed and published by Moomtain on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how that information is used, and your choices regarding that information.

By downloading or using SimLocation, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

Key takeaway: SimLocation does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to Moomtain's own servers. All user data remains on your device.

🔍 2. Information We Collect

SimLocation collects only the minimum information required to provide its core functionality.

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using Android SharedPreferences and a local database. It is never transmitted to Moomtain:

  • App settings: update frequency, simulation accuracy, safe mode status, timezone simulation preference, background service duration, simulated speed, direction, and altitude.
  • Favorite locations: GPS coordinates and custom names you save within the App.
  • Recent locations: locations you have recently selected or simulated.
  • Reward Beans balance: an in-app currency balance earned by watching rewarded ads.

2.2 Location Data

SimLocation requests access to your device's precise location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) and approximate location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION). This permission is used solely to:

  • Center the map on your current position when requested.
  • Provide context for searching and selecting simulation targets.

Your actual GPS coordinates are not uploaded to any server operated by Moomtain. The App's core feature — mock location — replaces location data reported to other apps via the Android mock location API; it does not read or transmit your real location externally.

2.3 Information Collected by Third Parties

The App integrates third-party SDKs that may collect data independently according to their own privacy policies:

SDK / Service Purpose Data Potentially Collected Privacy Policy
Google AdMob Display banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads Advertising ID, IP address, device info, interaction data policies.google.com/privacy
Google Maps SDK Render interactive maps Map tile requests, approximate location for geocoding policies.google.com/privacy

Moomtain does not control the data practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies for full details.

🔐 3. App Permissions

SimLocation requests the following Android permissions:

Permission Why It Is Needed
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Obtain precise GPS coordinates for centering the map and initiating mock location.
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION Fallback approximate location when fine GPS is unavailable.
ACCESS_MOCK_LOCATION Core feature: set a simulated GPS location for other apps via Android's mock location API.
INTERNET Load map tiles (Google Maps), load and display ads (AdMob), and perform location searches.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Check network availability before making network requests.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION Run the mock location service as a foreground service so it remains active while you use other apps.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Display a persistent notification showing the active simulation status and a stop control.
WAKE_LOCK Prevent the CPU from sleeping while the mock location service is running.
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS Allow users to optionally exempt the App from battery optimization to ensure stable background operation.

⚙️ 4. How We Use Information

The locally stored data described in Section 2.1 is used exclusively to:

  • Restore your preferred settings between sessions.
  • Display and manage your saved and recent locations.
  • Track your Reward Beans balance for the in-app rewards system.

Moomtain does not use any information for marketing, profiling, or analytics purposes on its own servers. Third-party SDKs (AdMob, Google Maps) may use collected data for their own purposes as described in their respective privacy policies.

📢 5. Advertising & Ad Personalization

SimLocation is a free app supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob. The App displays banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded interstitial ads.

Where required by applicable law (e.g., the EU General Data Protection Regulation — GDPR, or the California Consumer Privacy Act — CCPA), the App will present a consent dialog managed by the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) before showing personalized ads. You may update your ad consent preferences at any time via Settings → Privacy Settings inside the App.

If you choose not to consent to personalized ads, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized and less relevant to your interests.

🗑️ 6. Data Retention & Deletion

All data generated by SimLocation (settings, favorites, recent locations, Reward Beans) is stored locally on your device and retained until you choose to delete it.

You can delete all local data at any time via:

  • Settings → Delete All Data within the App, or
  • Android device settings: Settings → Apps → SimLocation → Storage → Clear Data.

Uninstalling the App will also remove all locally stored data. Moomtain has no ability to recover this data on your behalf.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

SimLocation is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

🛡️ 8. Data Security

Because all App-generated data remains on your device, it is protected by Android's standard application sandbox and your device's security measures (e.g., screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device's operating system and security patches up to date.

Moomtain cannot guarantee the security of data transmitted to or processed by third-party SDKs. Please refer to those providers' security documentation for more information.

⚖️ 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access & Portability: Since all data is stored locally, you can view it directly within the App.
  • Deletion: Delete all locally stored data as described in Section 6.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Adjust your ad consent settings in-app (Section 5).
  • Google's Ad Settings: Manage or reset your Google Advertising ID at adssettings.google.com.

For requests related to data held by Google (AdMob, Maps), please contact Google directly via their data request portals.
